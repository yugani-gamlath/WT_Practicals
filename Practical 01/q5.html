Chemistry: The Science of Matter

1. Chemical Elements

Chemical elements are the building blocks of matter. Each element is represented by a unique symbol:

H Hydrogen

He Helium

O Oxygen

C Carbon

2. Chemical Compounds

Chemical compounds are formed when elements combine in specific ratios. Some common compounds include:

Water: H2O

Carbon Dioxide: CO2

Ammonia: NH3

Methane: CH4

3. Periodic Table

The Periodic Table organizes elements by their properties. It provides valuable information about each element, such as atomic number and atomic mass.

4. Atomic Structure

Atoms are the smallest units of matter. They consist of:

Protons (positively charged)

Neutrons (neutral)

Electrons (negatively charged)

5. Conclusion

Chemistry is a fascinating field that helps us understand the substances that make up our world. It plays a crucial role in industries, medicine, and environmental science, among others.
